Pricing Conditions Example (Plain Text)
======================================

Overview
--------
This example shows typical purchasing pricing conditions used in SAP S/4HANA for procurement.[web:1]

Condition Record 1 – Material MAT-1001 with Vendor 10000001
-----------------------------------------------------------
Key
- Vendor: 10000001 - ABC Components Pvt Ltd
- Material: MAT-1001 - Laptop Computer 14 inch
- Purchasing Organization: 1000
- Plant: 1100
- Valid From: 2026-01-01
- Valid To: 2026-12-31

Conditions
- PB00 (Gross Price): 55,000.00 INR per EA
- RA01 (Rebate): 2.0 %
- FRA1 (Freight): 500.00 INR per delivery
- ZDS1 (Header Discount): 1.0 % for order value above 500,000.00 INR

Condition Record 2 – Material MAT-2005 with Vendor 10000001
-----------------------------------------------------------
Key

Condition Record 3 – Contract-Level Conditions (Material Group)
----------------------------------------------------------------
Key
- Vendor: 10000002 - Global IT Services Ltd
- Material Group: 0010 - IT Hardware
- Purchasing Organization: 1000
- Valid From: 2026-01-01
- Valid To: 2026-12-31

Conditions
- PB00 (Gross Price): As per item
- ZCNT (Contract Discount): 3.0 % on all IT hardware items
