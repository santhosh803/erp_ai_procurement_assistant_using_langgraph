Pricing Conditions Example
=======================================

Overview
--------
This example shows typical purchasing pricing conditions used in SAP S/4HANA
Cloud for procurement. Purchasing pricing is determined by a calculation schema
(condition technique): condition types (gross price, discounts, surcharges,
freight, taxes) are read from condition records by access sequence and combined
into the net and effective price. Condition records can be maintained at vendor/
material, vendor/material-group, contract, or info-record level.

Condition Record 1 - Material MAT-1001 with Supplier 10000001
-------------------------------------------------------------
Key
- Supplier (Business Partner): 10000001 - ABC Components Pvt Ltd
- Material: MAT-1001 - Laptop Computer 14 inch
- Purchasing Organization: 1000
- Plant: 1100
- Valid From: 2026-01-01
- Valid To: 2026-12-31

Conditions
- PB00 (Gross Price): 55,000.00 INR per EA
- RA01 (Discount % on Gross): 2.0 %
- FRA1 (Freight %): handled per delivery
- FRB1 (Freight Value): 500.00 INR per delivery
- ZDS1 (Header Discount): 1.0 % for order value above 500,000.00 INR

Condition Record 2 - Material MAT-2005 with Supplier 10000001
-------------------------------------------------------------
Key
- Supplier (Business Partner): 10000001 - ABC Components Pvt Ltd

- FRB1 (Freight Value): 0.00 INR (freight included)

Condition Record 3 - Contract-Level Conditions (Material Group)
---------------------------------------------------------------
Key
- Supplier (Business Partner): 10000002 - Global IT Services Ltd
- Material Group: 0010 - IT Hardware
- Purchasing Organization: 1000
- Valid From: 2026-01-01
- Valid To: 2026-12-31

Conditions
- PB00 (Gross Price): As per item / call-off
- ZCNT (Contract Discount): 3.0 % on all IT hardware items
- ZPAY (Payment Discount): additional 1.0 % for payment within 10 days

Condition Record 4 - Service Conditions (Lean Services)
-------------------------------------------------------
Key
- Supplier (Business Partner): 10000002 - Global IT Services Ltd
- Service Material: MAT-3010 - On-site Hardware Installation Service
- Purchasing Organization: 1000
- Valid From: 2026-01-01
- Valid To: 2026-12-31

Conditions
- PB00 (Gross Price): 1,200.00 INR per HUR (hour)
- ZSVC (Service Volume Discount): 5.0 % for confirmed effort >= 40 HUR per month

Tax Note
--------
- Output for India typically applies GST (e.g., 18%) determined via tax
  condition types and the relevant tax code at invoice; tax is calculated on the
  net value after purchasing discounts/surcharges.

Notes
-----
- Pricing condition records are maintained via the Manage Purchasing Info
  Records and condition-maintenance Fiori apps; contracts (quantity/value) carry
  their own conditions that POs call off against.
- Effective price (net price minus cash discount plus delivery costs) is used by
  source-of-supply comparison and supplier evaluation analytics.
