Material Master Sample
===================================

Overview
--------
This sample illustrates core material master fields used in Sourcing and
Procurement in SAP S/4HANA Cloud (Public Edition). In SAP S/4HANA the material
master is maintained per organizational level (client, plant, storage location,
valuation area) and is grouped into views such as Basic Data, Purchasing, MRP,
and Accounting. A "product type" distinguishes physical materials from services;
service items are increasingly modeled with Lean Services, which is SAP's
strategic direction for service procurement.

Material MAT-1001
------------------
Basic Data
- Material Number: MAT-1001
- Description: Laptop Computer 14 inch
- Product Type: Material (physical)
- Base Unit of Measure: EA
- Material Group: 0010 - IT Hardware
- Division: 00

Purchasing Data
- Purchasing Group: 001 - IT Procurement
- Order Unit: EA
- Purchasing Value Key: STD
- Planned Delivery Time (Days): 7
- Standard Purchase Order Quantity: 5
- Automatic PO Allowed: Yes
- GR-Based Invoice Verification: Yes

Plant Data (Plant 1100)
- Plant: 1100
- Storage Location: 0001
- MRP Type: PD (MRP)
- Lot Size: EX (lot-for-lot)
- Reorder Point: 5 EA

Accounting / Valuation Data (Valuation Area 1100)
- Valuation Class: 3100 - Trading Goods
- Price Control: V (Moving Average)
- Standard / Moving Average Price: 55,000.00 INR

Material MAT-3010 (Service)
---------------------------
Basic Data
- Material Number: MAT-3010
- Description: On-site Hardware Installation Service
- Product Type: Service (Lean Services)
- Base Unit of Measure: HUR (hour)
- Material Group: 0090 - IT Services
- Division: 00

Purchasing Data
- Purchasing Group: 001 - IT Procurement
- Order Unit: HUR
- Service Period: defined via Start Date and End Date on the requisition/PO item
  (services are consumed over a period, not on a single delivery date)
- Confirmation Required: Service Entry Sheet / lean service confirmation before
  invoice posting
- GR-Based Invoice Verification: Yes (service confirmation acts as the receipt)

Account Assignment
- Account Assignment Category: K (Cost Center) by default for indirect services
- Valuation: non-stock; cost posted directly to the assigned account object
