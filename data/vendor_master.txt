Supplier (Business Partner) Master Sample
======================================================

Overview
--------
This sample illustrates supplier master data for SAP S/4HANA Cloud Sourcing and
Procurement. In SAP S/4HANA the legacy "vendor master" is unified under the
Business Partner (BP) model: a single Business Partner is created once and
assigned the Supplier role, then extended with company-code (financial) and
purchasing-organization data. The Manage Business Partner Master Data Fiori apps
are used to create and maintain these records.

Notes
-----
- Supplier evaluation (quality, delivery, price, service) feeds the Supplier
  Management line of business and supports source determination.
- Bank details, withholding-tax, and classification data are maintained on the
  same Business Partner; one BP can hold both Customer and Supplier roles.
- AI assistance (Joule) can surface supplier information conversationally, e.g.
  "show purchase orders for supplier 10000001".
